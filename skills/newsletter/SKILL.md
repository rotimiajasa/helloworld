# Newsletter

## Format

```
Subject: {{the most interesting hook from this week's articles}}

Hi friend,

[1 short opener — a Naija-specific observation or a number that surprised me]

Three new pieces this week:

1. {{title 1}} — {{1 sentence why}}
   {{url}}

2. {{title 2}} — {{1 sentence why}}
   {{url}}

3. {{title 3}} — {{1 sentence why}}
   {{url}}

[Optional: one short "tip of the week" — 2-3 sentences, NOT a sales pitch]

If this was useful, forward it to one friend who's tired of "make money online" listicles. That's the whole growth strategy.

— [Author]
P.S. Reply to this email with what you'd like covered next.
```

## Rules
- 400–600 words MAX.
- One emoji max for the entire email (and only if natural).
- No "Hey [first name]" — keep it warm but not corporate.
- Each article gets ≤ 25 words of teaser.
- One soft CTA only (forward to a friend OR reply with feedback). Never both in same email.
- Naira figures get the ₦ symbol.
