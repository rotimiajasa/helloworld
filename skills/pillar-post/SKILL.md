# Pillar Post

A pillar post is the canonical resource on a topic. Other posts link INTO it. It must:

- Cover the topic exhaustively but readably (clear H2/H3, lots of lists/tables).
- Use a Table of Contents at the top (markdown anchors).
- Include a comparison table where relevant.
- Reference at least 3 authoritative external sources.
- Include a "Common mistakes" section.
- End with a 5-Q FAQ block + JSON-LD FAQPage schema.

## Section template

```
# {{title}}

> 2-sentence hook. State the reader's pain or a counter-intuitive claim.

## TL;DR
- bullet 1
- bullet 2
- bullet 3

## Table of Contents
- [Section 1](#section-1)
- ...

## Why this matters in Nigeria right now
2-4 paragraphs of context with current Naira numbers.

## {{H2 sections — 5 to 9 of them}}
Use real examples, screenshots placeholders `![alt](placeholder.png)`,
real Naira/USD figures, and link to official sources.

## Common mistakes
- mistake 1
- mistake 2
- mistake 3

## Real numbers (callout)
> A reader earning $X on Upwork pays Y in fees, sees Z in their GTBank.
[YOUR-STORY: insert one sentence of personal experience here]

## Why this might not work for you
Honest 3-bullet caveats.

## Next steps
1. Concrete action.
2. Concrete action.
3. Concrete action.

## FAQ
**Q: ...**
A: ...

## Sources
- [Source 1](url)
- [Source 2](url)
```

## Writing checklist

- [ ] First 200 words: NO affiliate links.
- [ ] At least 1 real Naira number with source.
- [ ] At least 2 [YOUR-STORY] placeholders for human pass.
- [ ] At least 3 internal links (filled in by SEO editor).
- [ ] FAQ JSON-LD block at the end.
- [ ] Reading grade ≤ 8.
