# How-To Post

## Section template

```
# How to {{action}} in Nigeria — step-by-step ({{year}})

> 1-sentence promise: what the reader will have done by the end.

## What you'll need
- Item 1 (with link if it's a product/service)
- Item 2
- Time required: ~X minutes
- Cost: ₦X / $X

## Step 1: {{verb-led title}}
Concrete instruction. Screenshot placeholder. What success looks like.

## Step 2: …
…

## Step N: {{final verification step}}
How to confirm it worked.

## Troubleshooting
| Problem | Fix |
|---|---|
| Error message X | … |
| Stuck at step Y | … |

## What to do next
1. …
2. …

## FAQ (3 questions, JSON-LD)
```

## Writing checklist

- [ ] Steps numbered, each with one clear action verb.
- [ ] At least one "verify it worked" check per critical step.
- [ ] Troubleshooting table with at least 3 known issues.
- [ ] No affiliate link inside step instructions — only in "What you'll need".
- [ ] Mention Nigerian-specific gotchas (NIN, BVN, account-name mismatch, etc.).
