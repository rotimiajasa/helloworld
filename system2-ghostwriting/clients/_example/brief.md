## Who they are
- **Name**: Jane Doe
- **Role**: Co-founder & CEO, Acme Health (Lagos)
- **LinkedIn**: https://linkedin.com/in/janedoe
- **Followers at start**: 4,200
- **Tier**: Pro ($499/mo)
- **Start date**: 2026-05-01

## Their business
Acme Health sells a SaaS platform to private hospitals in Nigeria and Kenya. Series A, ~30 employees. Customers: hospital admins.

## Their goal (90-day)
Generate 5 inbound demo requests/month from LinkedIn + position herself as a thought leader on African healthtech.

## ICP for their content (who should read every post)
- Hospital admins, medical directors, hospital owners in Sub-Saharan Africa
- Healthtech investors looking at Africa
- Senior engineers/PMs they want to recruit

## Taboo topics
- Specific competitor names (never mention X, Y, Z by name)
- Religion, politics, COVID conspiracy adjacents
- Anything about cap table or fundraising specifics

## Topics they want covered
- Operational realities of African hospitals (their differentiator vs. WHO data dumps)
- Bringing tech to non-English-fluent staff
- Hiring engineering talent in Lagos vs. remote
- Their hot-take: "patient data sovereignty" matters more in Africa than EU

## Voice anti-patterns
- Don't sound like generic "founder Twitter" (Naval-isms, pithy aphorisms).
- Don't be salesy.
- Don't use "we're hiring!" as the entire post — bury those.

## Logistics
- Approval channel: WhatsApp voice notes
- Posting tool: Buffer (account in their hands)
- Approval cadence: Mon morning batch for the week
- She's most responsive 7-9am WAT and 8-10pm WAT
