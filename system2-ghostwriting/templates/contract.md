## 2. Term
Initial term: **3 months** from the effective date. Auto-renews monthly thereafter. Either party may cancel with **30 days written notice** after the initial term.

## 3. Fees
${{price}}/month, due on the 1st of each month. Net-7 from invoice. Late payment after 14 days pauses delivery.

## 4. Refunds
- No refund for posts already delivered/published.
- Pro-rated refund for unstarted month if cancelled within first 7 days of that month.

## 5. IP
- Client owns all delivered content upon payment.
- Provider may use anonymized excerpts (no client name) in marketing portfolio unless Client opts out in writing.

## 6. Confidentiality
Both parties keep confidential information confidential. Standard mutual NDA terms apply. Client may request a separate detailed NDA at no extra cost.

## 7. Out of scope
Provider is NOT responsible for:
- LinkedIn algorithm changes affecting reach.
- Content posted to other platforms (X, Instagram, etc.) — those are quoted separately.
- Any consequences from Client's ignoring Provider's advice on taboo topics.

## 8. AI disclosure
Provider uses AI tools (including Claude) in the drafting process. All content is reviewed and edited by Provider before delivery. Final voice and message reflect Client's input.
