# Onboarding Questionnaire

Sent immediately after contract signed. Client fills async; takes them ~15 min. Saves 20 min off the voice interview.

---

Hi {{first_name}} —

Welcome aboard. Before our voice interview on {{interview_date}}, please reply to this email with answers to whatever feels easy. Skip anything you'd rather discuss live.

## About you

1. **One-sentence bio** (the one you'd want in your LinkedIn header).
2. **Three websites/socials** that show what you do (link to company, personal site, X if relevant).
3. **What time zone are you in?** And what's the best response time we should aim for?

## About the offer

4. **Who's your perfect client/user/hire?** (Describe in one paragraph.)
5. **What's the ONE thing you want LinkedIn to do for you in the next 90 days?** (Pick one: pipeline, hiring, fundraise, exit, partnership, none-of-the-above.)
6. **What's been holding you back from posting consistently?**

## Voice samples

7. **Paste 3 of your favorite posts you've already written** (LinkedIn, Twitter, blog — anything).
8. **Paste 1 long-form thing you wrote that you're proud of** (essay, founder update, internal memo).
9. **Send 1 voice note** (WhatsApp / Telegram / Voxer) explaining your business to me like I'm a smart friend. 60 seconds is plenty. *This is the single most useful thing — it captures your real cadence.*

## Boundaries

10. **What 3 topics are completely off-limits?** (Religion, politics, specific competitors, family, anything.)
11. **What 3 topics do you wish someone wrote about more in your space?**
12. **Is there anyone whose content I should ABSOLUTELY NOT make you sound like?** (Your style anti-pattern.)

## Logistics

13. **Where do you want drafts?** (Email / Slack DM / WhatsApp / Notion shared doc — your choice.)
14. **Where do you currently post from?** (Buffer / Typefully / native LinkedIn — we'll match.)
15. **Anything I haven't asked that I should know?**

---

Reply when ready — no formatting needed, voice notes welcome for any answer.

Looking forward to it.

— {{your_name}}
