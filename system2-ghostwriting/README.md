# System 2 — AI-Powered LinkedIn Ghostwriting

A productized service. We sell 12–20 LinkedIn posts per month + 2 newsletters to African tech founders/operators who want to build a personal brand but can't write consistently. Claude does ~90% of the work; you spend ≤1 hour/day on sales calls, approvals, and client check-ins.

## Why this hits $500/mo in 60 days

- **Pricing**: $299 (Starter, 12 posts/mo) / $499 (Pro, 20 posts + monthly newsletter) / $899 (Founder, 25 posts + LinkedIn carousel templates + reply triage).
- **Math**: 2 Starter clients = $598/mo. **One** Pro client + **one** Starter = $798/mo.
- **Realistic timeline** (see [`PLAYBOOK.md`](./PLAYBOOK.md)):
  - Week 1: Setup + portfolio.
  - Week 2–3: Outbound + 1st client signed (~$299).
  - Week 4–6: 2nd client signed.
  - Week 7–8: 3rd client.

## ICP (who we sell to)

- **Primary**: African (Nigerian, Kenyan, South African, Ghanaian) early/mid-stage tech founders, operators (heads of growth/product/engineering), VCs, and consultants who:
  - Already post on LinkedIn ≥2×/month (proves intent).
  - Have <30k followers (still in growth phase, willing to invest).
  - Run a B2B business or want to attract one.
  - Active in ecosystems: YC, Techstars, FFFA, Antler, AfricArena.
- **Secondary**: US/UK-based diaspora founders running African-market businesses (high willingness to pay).

## What Claude does end-to-end

| Workflow | Claude | You (human) |
|---|---|---|
| **Prospecting** | Drafts 20 ICP-matched cold DMs/day from a target list | 5 min: skim/approve, hit send |
| **Sales call** | Pre-call brief, post-call summary, proposal | Run the 30-min Zoom call |
| **Onboarding** | Voice analysis from interview transcript → style guide | 60-min recorded interview, once per client |
| **Content gen** | 4 weekly posts/client, ranked best-of-3 | 15 min/client/week to approve & schedule |
| **Reply triage** | Categorize comments/DMs, draft replies | 10 min/day to send approved replies |
| **Reporting** | Monthly metrics report w/ commentary | 0 min — auto-emailed |
| **Renewal/upsell** | Drafts upsell email at month 3 | 5 min to personalize |

## Money flow

| Source | Rail | Settles to |
|---|---|---|
| Client pays $299–$899/mo | Stripe Atlas (US LLC) OR Wise USD account OR Paystack USD | Wise USD → Wise NGN → Nigerian bank, 1–2 days |
| Alternative: USDT | Binance Pay or wallet | Binance P2P → Naira same-day |
| Nigerian client (rare) | Paystack (NGN) | Direct, next-day |

> **Tip**: You can operate without a US LLC at first using Wise Business Account (accepts USD ACH/wire from Stripe via Stripe Atlas requirement OR direct from clients). Long-term, opening a Stripe Atlas LLC ($500 one-time) unlocks Stripe Checkout — which closes ~30% more deals than "send me an invoice."

## Risk & mitigation

- **Slow first client** → Have a backup channel: list service on Contra and Upwork as "AI-augmented LinkedIn ghostwriter." Lower margin but faster first sale.
- **Client churn** → 3-month minimum in contract. Monthly metrics report (auto-generated) demonstrates ROI and reduces cancellations.
- **Quality drift** → Every post is human-reviewed before scheduling. The `voice-extract.ts` re-runs every 60 days to refresh the style guide as the client's voice evolves.
- **LinkedIn DM limits** → Stay <20 DMs/day per account to avoid throttling. Prospect.ts enforces this.
