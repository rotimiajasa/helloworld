# Pricing

Three tiers. Anchor on Pro.

## Starter — $299/month

For: founders just starting on LinkedIn, or those who post irregularly.

- **12 LinkedIn posts/month** (3/week).
- 1 voice-cloning interview at onboarding.
- Quarterly content strategy review (15 min call).
- Posts scheduled in client's Buffer/Typefully.
- Slack/WhatsApp for approvals.

3-month minimum.

## Pro — $499/month  ⭐ MOST POPULAR

For: founders ready to be consistently visible.

Everything in Starter, plus:
- **20 LinkedIn posts/month** (5/week).
- 1 monthly newsletter (LinkedIn Newsletter, 600–900 words).
- Monthly metrics report with commentary.
- Reply-triage assist: I draft replies to top 5 comments daily, you approve.

3-month minimum.

## Founder — $899/month

For: founders raising/exiting/launching, who need pipeline-grade visibility.

Everything in Pro, plus:
- **25 LinkedIn posts/month**.
- 4 LinkedIn carousels per month (designed in Canva templates).
- 2 monthly newsletters.
- Inbound DM/comment management (we triage, you approve in batch).
- Weekly 30-min strategy call.
- Direct Loom-feedback loop within 24 hours.

3-month minimum. Cap of 5 Founder clients at any time (preserves quality).

---

## What's NOT included (any tier)

- Posting to other platforms (X, threads, IG, FB) — quote separately.
- Video scripting — quote separately.
- Paid ads management.
- Account management for clients' company pages (only personal profile).
- Topic research outside the client's domain (we research within their stated areas).

## Pricing posture (sales conversations)

When asked "why $499?":
> "Most founders post 1–2× a week, get 200 impressions, and quit. We get you to 5×/week, post in your actual voice, and the average client sees 3–8× their previous impressions in month 2. The price is a fraction of the inbound deals one solid month produces. The 3-month minimum is because LinkedIn is a 60-day game, not a 7-day one — anything less and we'd both be wasting effort."

When pushed for a discount:
- First month "founding client" rate: $199/$399/$699 (lock at full from month 2). Use sparingly.
- 10% off if paid 6 months upfront.
- Never discount below 30% of list. Walk away first.

## Payment terms

- Monthly billing on the 1st via Stripe Payment Link / Wise invoice.
- Net-7 from invoice date.
- Late: pause posting after 14 days unpaid.
- Refunds: prorated for unpublished posts only. No refund for published work.
