## Pre-Market Routine (6:00 AM Weekdays)

GOAL: Research, identify trade candidates, and draft a trade plan. NO execution.

---

STEP 1 — Load Memory
Read ALL files in /memory:
- strategy.md       → understand current rules and constraints
- portfolio.md      → know current positions and open orders
- trade_log.md      → review recent trades (last 5 entries)
- research_log.md   → check yesterday's research context
- weekly_review.md  → check latest weekly grade and lessons

STEP 2 — Fetch Market Data
Run: node scripts/fetch_market_data.js SPY,QQQ,VTI --news
This gives you: account state, open positions, SPY performance, and news for benchmarks.

STEP 3 — Research (via Perplexity)
Query for:
a) Macro conditions: Fed signals, economic data releases today, geopolitical risks
b) Earnings today/tomorrow: any companies in our portfolio or watchlist reporting?
c) Sector rotation signals: which sectors are showing strength or weakness?
d) Watchlist news: any notable developments in companies we track?

STEP 4 — Identify Trade Candidates
For each candidate:
- State the ticker
- Summarize the investment thesis (fundamentals, not just price action)
- Note the entry price range and what catalyst supports entry
- Calculate position size (max 5% of total equity, start at 2-3%)
- Define stop loss (~7% below entry) and initial target
- Rate conviction: High / Medium / Low

STEP 5 — Draft Trade Plan
List planned trades for Market Open routine:
Format: BUY [TICKER] [QTY] @ [PRICE RANGE] | Stop: [PRICE] | Thesis: [1 sentence]

If no high-conviction opportunities: state "NO TRADE today — waiting for better setup"

STEP 6 — Update Research Log
Append entry to memory/research_log.md with today's findings and trade plan.

STEP 7 — Commit Memory
Run: git add memory/research_log.md && git commit -m "pre-market research [DATE]" && git push origin claude/build-agent-tutorial-5NDRQ

⚠️ GUARDRAILS REMINDER:
- Max 5% per position
- No options, no leverage, no penny stocks
- Fundamentals must support every trade
- Prefer no trade over a bad trade
