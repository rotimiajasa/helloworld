# Trading Strategy

## Philosophy
Long-term / swing trading driven by fundamentals. We aim to outperform the S&P 500 over rolling 12-month periods by identifying high-quality companies trading at reasonable valuations.

## Core Principles
- Fundamentals over technicals: earnings growth, revenue trends, balance sheet strength
- Prefer no trade over a bad trade
- Diversification across sectors — no single thesis dominates

## Entry Criteria
- Strong or improving revenue/earnings growth (YoY)
- Reasonable P/E relative to growth (PEG < 2 preferred)
- Positive macro tailwinds for the sector
- Clear investment thesis documented before entry

## Exit Criteria
- Position down > 7% from entry → cut loss
- Trailing stop ~10% from recent high (set at market open)
- Fundamental thesis has broken (e.g., earnings miss + guidance cut)
- Position hits target price / becomes significantly overvalued

## Position Sizing
- Max 5% of portfolio per position
- Start new positions at 2–3%, add to winners
- Never go above 5% in a single name

## Prohibited
- Options, futures, leveraged ETFs
- High-frequency / scalp trading
- Pure technical speculation without fundamental basis
- Penny stocks / micro-caps under $500M market cap

## Benchmark
- S&P 500 (SPY) — compare performance weekly and quarterly

## Learning
- Document every trade decision and outcome
- Update this file when new patterns are identified
- Review and revise quarterly based on weekly_review.md findings
