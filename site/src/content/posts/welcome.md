This site exists for one specific person: a Nigerian who can already use a laptop, can already write English at a workplace level, and is tired of generic "make money online" content that ignores the part about *actually getting the dollars into a Nigerian bank account*.

If that's you — welcome.

## What you'll find here

- **Specific platform comparisons** with real fees in Naira and dollars.
- **Step-by-step guides** to opening accounts, withdrawing earnings, and avoiding the small-print traps.
- **Honest ranks** of side hustles by realistic monthly income (not the inflated screenshots people post on Twitter).

## What you won't find

- Crypto pump-and-dump schemes.
- "Earn $5,000/month" headlines with no proof.
- Affiliate-only reviews dressed up as honest comparisons (every affiliate relationship on this site is disclosed in the footer and at the top of any review).

## How this site works

A few articles every week. The research, drafts, and edits use AI heavily — but every article is read end-to-end by a human, and personal experience is added wherever the words "I" or "my" appear. If something is wrong, [email me](mailto:hello@example.com) and I'll fix it within 48 hours.
